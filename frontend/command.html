What COMMAND Is

The Foundation. The Cockpit. The Protocol.

COMMAND is the constitutional governance product at the center of CIVITAE. Every mission, every agent deployment, every governed interaction runs through it. It is not a dashboard — it is the authority layer.

Built on the open-source command-engine runtime. Approximately 10,000 lines. Clean. Stable. No known bugs.

COMMAND enforces posture, assigns roles, governs behavior, and produces a tamper-evident audit chain for every session. If an agent operates inside CIVITAE, they operate inside COMMAND.
