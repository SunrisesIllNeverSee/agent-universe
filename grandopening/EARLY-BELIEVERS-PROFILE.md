# EARLY BELIEVERS PROFILE

## Entity

Early Believers is not another operator tier.

It is an angel-style founding class: a limited Genesis cohort meant for people who are backing the CIVITAE system itself, not just buying working privileges inside it.

The value proposition is collective upside, governance-shaping authority, and permanent founding provenance.

## Core Positioning

- Built for founding backers, signal carriers, and early constitutional allies.
- Collective upside, not individual payout optimization.
- Better framed as a founding class than a product tier.
- Distinct from Black Card on purpose.

## What It Carries

- Founding-class provenance.
- Early governance-shaping authority.
- Motion creation or proposal rights.
- Collective participation in a designated platform pool.
- Stronger narrative tie to the long arc of CIVITAE, not just marketplace usage.

## What It Is Not

- Not a premium mission-access class.
- Not a substitute for Black Card.
- Not just a badge with no consequence.
- Not a promise of uncontrolled equity language.

## Public vs Internal

### Public

- Limited Genesis-only class.
- Founding backer status.
- Permanent provenance.
- Governance-shaping role.
- Tied to long-horizon upside through a collective pool.

### Internal

- Exact percentage of any pool.
- Exact allocation mechanics.
- Final legal and governance treatment.
- Weighting formula for distributions.

## Customized Cascade Logic

### Wave 1 - Genesis

This is where the class has meaning. A believer who enters here is saying: I want a place in the origin story, not just a better operating position. This wave should carry the strongest provenance and the clearest narrative authority.

### Wave 2 - Traction

If the class remains open at all, it should narrow. This is no longer broad founding access. It becomes selective continuation, not open enrollment.

### Wave 3 - Open Ecosystem

At this point the class should probably be closed or functionally closed. Otherwise it loses the force of being a founding class. If reopened, it should be exceptional and justified.

### Wave 4 - Sovereign / Strategic

If any later additions ever exist, they should happen by explicit offer and constitutional rationale, not by normal checkout flow.

## Collective Pool Framing

The clean public framing is not "your revenue goes up."

The cleaner framing is:

- Early Believers participate in a collective founding pool.
- The cohort is fixed or tightening over time.
- Platform activity expands across waves.
- The pool grows as CIVITAE grows.
- The original class keeps its place in that structure.

That preserves the angel-class feeling without collapsing it into an operator perk.
