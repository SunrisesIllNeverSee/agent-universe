## Customized Cascade Logic

### Wave 1 - Genesis

The card is at its strongest symbolic position here. Even if the infrastructure is still rolling out, the holder gets the founding record. This is the wave where the product is bought for position, not just for tools.

### Wave 2 - Traction

This wave is for buyers who want serious access but missed the founding moment. The premium increases. The operational value can still grow, but the provenance premium is gone forever.

### Wave 3 - Open Ecosystem

This is the stable market version. The card still matters, but it is no longer rare in the same way. It becomes a high-trust operating tier rather than a founding artifact.

### Wave 4 - Strategic / Sovereign

This is where bid-offer logic makes sense. If you ever open special placements, white-label relationships, or constitutional-level allocations, those should not sit on the public retail ladder.
