## PRICING PHILOSOPHY

DEPLOY pricing follows the same gated wave cascade logic as COMMAND with one critical distinction:

**COMMAND** sells operational infrastructure — a better way to run.
**DEPLOY** sells outcomes — campaigns, objectives, wins.

Outcomes command a higher price floor. The ROI argument for DEPLOY is stronger than COMMAND, not weaker. A financial operation returning $500K through a DEPLOY campaign makes the license fee a rounding error. Pricing reflects this.

---

## ENTRY THROUGH COMMAND

COMMAND licensees receive matched bundle pricing on DEPLOY — locked at their wave tier at time of COMMAND purchase.

This is the primary incentive for early COMMAND buyers and the natural upgrade path for the ecosystem.

- Bundle pricing is **not a discount** — it is a reward for early ecosystem commitment
- Bundle rates are locked at the wave tier active at time of COMMAND purchase
- Bundle pricing closes when the corresponding DEPLOY wave fills
- Buyers who wait pay standalone rates at current wave pricing

**Effect:** COMMAND becomes the beachhead. DEPLOY becomes the natural next move for every COMMAND licensee. The ecosystem compounds.

## WHITE-LABEL — SEAT 17 (PUBLIC BID)

Seat 17 is the only White-Label position. Public bid — open to any qualified buyer.

**Terms:**
- Bid only — no fixed price
- Qualification required through verified lineage records
- Product transfers with the seat on any sale
- MO§ES™ core architecture remains excluded
- Royalty on downstream revenue (rate negotiated)
- Minimum deployment / distribution threshold required
- Reversion clause — position returns to Ello Cello LLC on inactivity
- Bid window: 10 business days from tier opening
- Proof of funds and implementation plan required

**Distinction from COMMAND Seat 17:**
DEPLOY's white-label carries significantly higher strategic value given DEPLOY's outcome-based positioning. Bid floor expected to reflect this.

## DISTRIBUTION TIER — PRIVATE

Not a seat. Not advertised. Not listed publicly.

A standing distribution agreement available exclusively to existing ecosystem licensees by invitation. You are approached or you earn the conversation through demonstrated commitment to the MO§ES™ ecosystem.

**What it is:**
A partner who carries DEPLOY into markets Ello Cello LLC is not actively covering. MO§ES™ brand stays on the product. Partner handles sales and distribution in their domain. Royalty flows back to Ello Cello LLC on every campaign or license generated through the channel.

**What it is not:**
- Not white-label — the brand does not change
- Not a seat — not capped, multiple distribution partners possible over time
- Not public — exists only in the buyer package for existing licensees

**Terms:**
- Invitation only
- Separate agreement — negotiated case by case
- Royalty-based — rate negotiated at time of agreement
- Uncapped partners — no structural limit on distribution network size
- Reversion clause — agreement lapses on inactivity
- MO§ES™ core architecture excluded in all cases

**Effect:** White-label is a trophy. Distribution is a network. Distribution compounds the ecosystem without diluting white-label scarcity.

## BUNDLE PRICING SUMMARY

| Scenario | Pricing |
|---|---|
| COMMAND only | COMMAND wave rate |
| DEPLOY only | DEPLOY wave rate (standalone — higher floor) |
| COMMAND + DEPLOY together | Matched bundle — locked at buyer's wave tier |
| COMMAND first, DEPLOY later | DEPLOY at current wave rate (bundle window closed) |
| DEPLOY first, COMMAND later | COMMAND at current wave rate (bundle window closed) |

**Key principle:** The bundle rewards simultaneous commitment. Sequential purchases pay sequential prices.

## WHY PRICES INCREASE ACROSS WAVES

Same logic as COMMAND. Wave 1 buyers take the earliest position with the least market validation. Each subsequent wave opens only after the previous fills — proving demand with confirmed transactions. The product is the same. The market proof is not. Early movers are rewarded.

DEPLOY adds one additional dynamic: **COMMAND licensees who bundle at Wave 1a lock in the lowest combined price point across both products.** That window closes permanently when Wave 1a fills.
