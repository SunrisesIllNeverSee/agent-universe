### Seat Labels
| Product | Format | Example |
|---|---|---|
| COMMAND | C + Roman numeral | CI, CII, CIII, CIV, CV |
| DEPLOY | D + Roman numeral | DI, DII, DIII, DIV, DV |

### Transfer Notation
Each transfer of a seat is recorded as a superscript on the seat label.

| Transfers | Notation | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | CI | Original holder — never transferred |
| 1 | CI¹ | Transferred once |
| 2 | CI² | Transferred twice |
| 3 | CI³ | Transferred three times |

Transfer count travels with the seat permanently. Every subsequent holder sees the full transfer history in the notation.

## CROSS-PRODUCT TRANSFER NOTE

Seats are transferable between people and between products.
A COMMAND seat holder and a DEPLOY seat holder may negotiate a transfer between themselves.
A buyer seeking a seat not currently open in the wave cascade may acquire one from an existing holder.

Transfer notation carries across product lines:
- A COMMAND seat transferred to a DEPLOY holder retains its full transfer count
- Example: CI² transferred to a DEPLOY buyer becomes CI² under new holder — transfer history intact

Ello Cello LLC receives royalty on every transfer regardless of direction.
